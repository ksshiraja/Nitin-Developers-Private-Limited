ABOUT GOA

India's tourism capital has witnessed unprecedented growth in the Real Estate sector in the past 5 years.
This was on account of the impetus gained by the local economy. Many Goan fans decide to buy a home for their annual vacations and spend longer periods here.

Source:www.lonelyplanet.com

  • Beaches

    Goa is lavishly gifted by nature for its scenic beauty, virgin beaches girdling its 105-km long palm-fringed coast interspersed with enchanting coves, bays and estuaries, tiny picturesque villages and above all, friendly and hospitable people.

  • Tourism

    Commonly known as "Pearl of the Orient" and a "Tourist Paradise", both in India and abroad, the state of Goa is located on the western coast of India in the coastal belt known as Konkan. The magnificent scenic beauty and the architectural splendors of its temples, churches and old houses have made Goa a firm favorite with travelers around the world.

  • Culture

    Goa has a soul which goes deep into unique history, rich culture and some of the prettiest natural scenery that India has to offer. Having been the meeting point of races, religions and cultures of East and West over the centuries, Goa has a multi-hued and distinctive lifestyle quite different from the rest of India.
