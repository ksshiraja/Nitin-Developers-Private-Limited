LE COMMERZ

Le Commerz is an independent stand alone Commercial building in the heart of Porvorim just Opposite the O Coqueiro restaurant. The building with a swanky showroom on the ground floor has a huge frontage on National Highway No. 17 on one side and the famous Chogum road on the other side, ideal for restaurants, car showroom etc.
