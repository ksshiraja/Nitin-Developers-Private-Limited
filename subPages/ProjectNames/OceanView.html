OCEAN VIEW

As the name suggests, Ocean View overlooks the Arabian Sea which is just at a stones throw. This beautiful residential apartment building is located in Dona Paula. It was constructed in 2005 and has semi detached Bungalows, combination of 2 BHK apartments, 3 BHK apartments and pent houses, all having a spectacular Sea view. One can see the cruise liners traveling from around the world and docking at the Vasco port. One can also have a beautiful sight of the famous Dona Paula jetty from all the apartments.
