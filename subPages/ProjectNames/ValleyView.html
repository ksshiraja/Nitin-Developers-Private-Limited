VALLEY VIEW

Valley View, Nitin Developers' debutante project started in the year 2004 at Dona Paula, one of the most posh residential areas of GOA. The building located on Nagali Hill, has a combination of 2 BHK and 3 BHK apartments with a Pent house, all overlooking the beautiful valley and village of Talegaon. From all the apartments one can also have a glimpse of the Miramar seafront and the Mandovi river. The project is just 50 mtrs from the proposed Rajiv Gandhi IT Park. The project is completed and sold.
