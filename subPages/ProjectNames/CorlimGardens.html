CORLIM GARDENS

"CORLIM GARDEN" CORLIM GARDEN DEFINES A WHOLE NEW STANDARD IN LUXURY LIVING AT AFFORDABLE PRICES The relaxed and serene province of Corlim, Goa presents a unique destination to those desiring a peaceful life. Corlim Gardens is a gated community of residences that treats you to Goa’s rustic charm without distancing you from the conveniences and essentials of the city. The picture- perfect mélange of a dreamy, bucolic setting and the vibrant cityscape, Corlim Gardens is a world where a lifetime of joys and happiness lie in store for you. A LANDMARK THAT BALANCES YOUR DESIRE FOR LUXURY WITH YOUR NEED FOR LEISURE WITHOUT DISTANCING YOU FROM THE CITY. With tastefully styled apartments spread amidst a large multi-activity landscape, Corlim Gardens welcomes you into a lifestyle that’s comforting when you’re indoors and exciting when you’re outdoors. The 2 BHK homes are spacious and equipped with luxury amenities. The landscape area offers a wide choice of activities for a relaxed or active outdoor life.
